Final Prototype with Lights

Before I introducted my making process, I would like to demonstrate the final prototype firstly. Because I really like the feeling of interwaved light and shadow, I put light in the object when I shoot. In my eyes, light brings life to still objects.

Another Shot Angle

I changed the composition and took another photo, which better showed the structure of the object.

CAD Flat Structure

Before laser cutting, I needed a vector image, which I designed and made with Rhino 6. Since I majored in industrial design during my undergraduate study, I am quite familiar with this software, and this step does not take too much time.

Software Setting

I set the power and the speed of laser-cutting machine by the corresonding software.

Laser Cutting

After laser cutting, I got the flat module as shown in the figure.

Assembly

The final step is assembly! While assembling, I found that the connections were a little shaky in some places. In future designs, I need to pay more attention to tolerances.
